🧠 Learning Objectives

  • • Identify major winter constellations (Orion, Taurus, Pleiades).
  • • Correlate star color with temperature and age.
  • • Model the life cycles of medium vs. massive stars.
  • • Solve the Apparent Brightness vs. Distance paradox.

Observational Protocols

Pattern Recognition

Sketch shapes for Orion and Taurus. Identify the brightest core stars.

Thermal Analysis

Categorize stars by color (Temperature indicators). Identify Red Supergiants vs Blue Giants.

Life Cycle Intel

Record data on star lifespan. Listen for the 'Death of a Star' auditory cues.
